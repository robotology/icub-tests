\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
This tests checks the position\+Direct control, sending a sinusoidal reference signal, with parametric frequency and amplitude. 

The sample time, typical in the range of 10 ms, can be also be adjusted by the user. This test currently does not return any error report. It simply moves a joint, and the user visually evaluates the smoothness of the performed trajectory. In the future automatic checks/plots may be added to the test. Be aware theat may exists set of parameters (e.\+g. high values of sample time / ampiltude /frequency) that may lead to PID instability and damage the joint. The test is able to check all the three types of yarp methods (single joint, multi joint, all joints), depending on the value of cmd\+Mode Parameter.
